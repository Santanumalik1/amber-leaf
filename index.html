About Amber Leaf

Amber Leaf was created around a simple idea: most B2B service businesses don’t need more dashboards, funnels or campaigns – they need more of the right conversations at the right time.

Instead of adding another complex tool to your stack, we build lean, niche websites that show up exactly when founders and operators are already searching for help. Our job is to meet that intent, capture the enquiry and hand it over to you without friction.

We never compete with you, resell your services or interfere with your client relationships. Amber Leaf stays behind the scenes as the quiet engine that turns high-intent Google searches into sales calls for your business.
